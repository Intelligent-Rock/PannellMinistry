Our Mission

Guided by Christian values, WeNxt provides education, recreational, and mentorship programs for disadvantaged youth in Aurora, IL, and surrounding counties (Kane, Dupage, and Will). These programs are designed to develop our next generation of future leaders.

WeNxt offers educational, recreational, and leadership experiences throughout the year at multiple locations, events, and specialty programs. The programs are designed to nurture each of our youth's imagination, confidence, talents, and individual spirit.

As a youth outreach, WeNxt continues to respond to the changing needs and varying demographics in our local communities. The WeNxt youth outreach continues to be a safe haven for our youth while developing future leaders to pour into our schools, communities, and workforce.

Though our programs and services continue to change and evolve to meet the demands of the community, our commitment and dedication to service and those we serve remain constant.

Programs:

Youth Mentorship

The youth mentorship program consists of partnering our youth with successful men and women to learn from, emulate, and develop lifelong beneficial relationships. The mentorship program breaks down barriers and allows our youth to feel love, learn self-discipline, and understand the responsibilities of being a good classmate, family member, and citizen. This program advocates the importance of staying away from drugs and gangs and focuses on providing an environment for them to reach their full potential as future leaders in their communities.

Social and Emotional Skills

At WeNxt, our youth learn to share with peers, manage conflict, solve problems independently, and nurture emotional intelligence. They take on age-appropriate challenges through group activities. Our youth make friends and build social-emotional skills for success inside and outside the classroom.
